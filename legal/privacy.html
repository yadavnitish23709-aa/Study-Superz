2. What Data We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Full Name — to personalise your experience
  • Email Address — for account, verification, and important updates
  • Password — stored as bcrypt hash (12 rounds). We never see your real password.
  • Class / Grade — to show relevant content

Collected Automatically

We Do NOT Collect

  • ❌ Your actual AI conversation content
  • ❌ Payment information (platform is free)
  • ❌ Precise geolocation
  • ❌ Camera, microphone, or biometric data
  • ❌ Social media profiles
  • ❌ Contacts or phone numbers

4. What We Will NEVER Do

These are firm commitments:

5. Where Data is Stored

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) — encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3)
  • Local Storage: Notes/preferences in your browser — never leaves your device unless you save to server
  • Logs: Server logs kept 30 days, then auto-deleted

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Until you request deletion + 30 days grace period
  • Server logs: 30 days maximum
  • Security logs: 90 days for abuse prevention
  • Session tokens: 15 min (access) / 7 days (refresh)
  • Password reset tokens: 1 hour
  • Email verification tokens: 24 hours

7. Third-Party Services
