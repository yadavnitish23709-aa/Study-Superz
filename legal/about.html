ℹ️ About Study Superz

Our mission, values, and the story behind India's most advanced free learning platform.

Founded: 2025 | Built with ❤️ in India

🎯 Our Mission

Study Superz was built with one goal: make world-class education free and accessible to every Indian student — from a village with slow internet to a JEE aspirant in a metro city.

Every student deserves access to AI tutors, 3D visualizations, animated lessons, and comprehensive study tools — regardless of their financial background.

🔧 Technology Stack

  • Frontend: Vanilla HTML/CSS/JS, Three.js, Plotly
  • Backend: Node.js + Express with full security middleware
  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security
  • AI: Anthropic Claude, Google Gemini, xAI Grok, DeepSeek
  • Music: Spotify Embed API + Web Audio API
  • Hosting: Vercel (frontend + serverless functions)
  • PWA: Service Worker + Web App Manifest
  • Android: Median.co APK wrapper

🌟 Platform Values

  • ✅ Free Forever — core features always free
  • ✅ No Ads — we don't show ads or sell your data
  • ✅ Privacy First — minimum data, maximum security
  • ✅ Honest AI — AI content clearly labelled
  • ✅ Inclusive — PWA works on low-end devices with offline support
